Why Pet Drug?

At Pet Drug, we are dedicated to promoting the health and well-being of pets. Our mission is to provide pet owners with access to high-quality drugs, vaccines, and supplements that are essential for their pets' overall health and happiness.

We believe that every pet deserves the best care. That's why we strive to offer a wide range of trusted products that meet the highest standards of safety, effectiveness, and affordability.

Our commitment to pet health extends beyond providing products. We aim to educate pet owners, empower them to make informed decisions, and support them in their journey to raise healthy and thriving pets.

Vaccines

Core vaccines

These vaccines are recommended for all pets, regardless of their lifestyle or exposure risk. They include vaccines against:



Canine parvovirus (CPV)




Distemper




Canine hepatitis (CH)




Rabies

Non-core vaccines

These vaccines are not recommended for all pets, but may be necessary for pets with certain lifestyle or exposure risks. They include vaccines against:



Bordetella bronchiseptica




Leptospirosis




Lyme disease

Combination vaccines

These vaccines combine multiple vaccines into a single shot, which can make it easier and more convenient to vaccinate your pet. Some common combination vaccines include:

DHLPP (distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis, parvovirus, and parainfluenza)

DHPP (distemper, hepatitis, parvovirus, and parainfluenza)

DAP (distemper, adenovirus, and parvovirus)

Check-Up Services

We have 4 core services on pet check-ups

Ensure the overall health and well-being of your pets with our comprehensive routine wellness check-up service. Our experienced veterinarians will conduct a thorough examination, including a physical assessment, temperature check, and evaluation of vital signs. This check-up allows us to detect early signs of illness, provide preventive care, and address any concerns you may have about your pets' health.

As pets age, their health needs change. Our senior pet check-up service is specifically designed to monitor and manage the health of older pets. Our veterinarians will perform a detailed examination, focusing on age-related conditions such as arthritis, dental health, organ function, and potential signs of cancer. Early detection and appropriate interventions can greatly enhance the quality of life for your senior pets.

If you're considering adopting a new pet, our pre-adoption check-up service ensures you make an informed decision and provide the best possible care for your new furry friend. Our veterinarians will conduct a thorough examination, review medical history (if available), perform necessary tests, and provide you with important insights about the pet's health, behavior, and any potential challenges you may face.

When your pet is feeling unwell or exhibiting concerning symptoms, our sick pet check-up service offers timely and accurate diagnosis to determine the underlying cause of their discomfort. Our veterinarians will perform a detailed examination, conduct diagnostic tests if needed, and develop an appropriate treatment plan to help your pet recover and regain their health.
